# Roles matrix

| Role | Intended holder | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Admin Safe | Multisig admin | Owns governance and admin-authorized configuration actions. |
| Treasury Safe | Treasury multisig | Receives or manages treasury-related funds and operational controls. |
| Operators | Manual signers / service accounts | Perform day-to-day operations after role assignment. |

Keep the real mapping in the local untracked `wallets.json` file.
